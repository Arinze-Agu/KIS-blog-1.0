The automated smart garbage collection system The automated smart dustbin system’s hardware design involves level and human presence detection components, messaging components, visual display component, sound and light indication component, password control keypad, and the microcontroller. The components which are the ultrasonic sensor, GSM module, LCD, LEDs, buzzer and keypad systems are connected to the microcontroller. The microcontroller is the control unit identifying the levels of waste in the bin and sending SMS to the authorities in charge of disposal and location of the wastebasket if any of the input detection devices is triggered. When the wastebasket reaches certain calibrated level, the GSM module will send an SMS to waste management authorities to notify them of the bin’s level. At the same time, the LCD located upon the wastebasket will display the level of waste in the bin in terms of written notification and progress bar. If the bin is full, it will lock automatically and remain locked until the waste management authorities come for disposal and issue the unlocking password.
